How to Maintain Your Hair Patch for Max Lifespan

By Expert Stylists • 5 Min Read

Getting a hair patch is an instant confidence booster, but to keep it looking entirely natural and feeling fresh over the months, proper maintenance is extremely important. If you treat your hair patch like your natural hair, taking some slight precautions, you can easily extend its lifespan up to 12 months or more.

1. Washing Your Hair Patch Correctly

Unlike natural growing hair, the hair on a patch doesn't receive oils from your scalp. This means it can dry out if washed too frequently with harsh chemicals.

  • Frequency: Wash your hair patch 1-2 times a week.
  • Products: Always use a sulfate-free, mild shampoo. Sulfates will strip the moisture from the artificial hair quickly.
  • Technique: Do not scrub vigorously. Gently pat the shampoo along the hair strokes in one direction (from root to tip) to avoid tangling.

2. The Importance of Conditioning

Conditioning is the secret to a long-lasting hair patch. Because the hair is not organically lubricated, you must manually lubricate it to keep it soft and shiny.

Apply a leave-in conditioner after every wash. We also highly recommend bringing the patch in for a deep-conditioning spa treatment at our Kolkata studio once every month.

3. Drying and Styling Tips

Heat is the enemy of artificial hair systems.

  • Towel Drying: Never rub the hair with a towel. Gently pat it dry.
  • Blow Drying: Let it air dry whenever possible. If you must blow-dry, use the coolest setting.
  • Brushing: Only brush the hair when it is completely dry or slightly damp using a wide-toothed comb or a specialized loop brush designed for wigs.

4. Sleeping With a Hair Patch

Friction directly damages the cuticles of the hair patch overnight.

To prevent this, use a silk or satin pillowcase. Cotton pillowcases pull and tug at the hair. If you have a longer hair patch style, lightly tying it up can prevent nasty morning tangles.

Expert Tip: Schedule Regular Maintenance

Regular monthly servicing is not just for cleaning your scalp and reapplying adhesives, but our experts also detangle, condition, and refresh the cuticles of the patch, adding literally months to its overall lifespan.

Remember, a hair patch is an investment in your confidence. With a few minor adjustments to your daily routine, you can ensure that you always look your absolute best.
