Hair Bonding FAQs

Is hair bonding safe for the scalp?

+
Yes, absolutely. We use dermatologically tested, FDA-approved adhesives that are completely skin-friendly and do not cause irritation to your scalp or damage your existing hair.

How long does the bonding last?

+
The adhesive grip typically lasts for 3 to 5 weeks depending on your lifestyle, sweating, and scalp type. You will need to visit our Kolkata studio for monthly maintenance (re-bonding and cleaning).

Can I wash my hair normally?

+
Yes, you can wash, comb, and style your hair exactly like you would with your natural hair. We will provide specific instructions and recommend shampoos to increase the lifespan of your hair patch.
