A secure, woven track system for adding volume and length without adhesives.

What is Hair Weaving?

Hair weaving is an excellent non-surgical hair restoration technique for men seeking a secure, glue-free option. As a top kolkata hair wig shop, it involves creating a small track or braid using your existing hair out of which a customized hair wig for men or patch (or weft) is sewn.

Because the hair system is physically woven into your natural hair, it provides a very secure hold. You can comfortably engage in rigorous activities like sports or swimming without worrying about the patch coming loose.

Benefits of Hair Weaving

  • Glue-Free Option: Ideal for men with extremely sensitive skin or adhesive allergies.
  • Extremely Secure: The woven track ensures the system won't shift during physical activities.
  • Natural Airflow: Allows your scalp to breathe more easily compared to full-coverage adhesive patches.
  • Seamless Blend: Real human hair is woven in to perfectly match your remaining hair by the experts at our wig shop in kolkata.
