✦ Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Get answers to the most common questions about hair fixing, pricing, maintenance, and results.

What is the price of hair patch in Kolkata?

+
The price of a hair patch in Kolkata at our studio starts from ₹5,999, depending on the type, hair density, material quality, and customization required. We offer both synthetic and 100% natural human hair options. Book a free consultation and we'll recommend the best option that fits your budget and needs.

Is hair fixing painful? Are there any side effects?

+
Absolutely not. Hair fixing is 100% non-surgical, painless, and has zero side effects. We use skin-friendly, FDA-approved adhesives and hypoallergenic materials. There's no surgery, no anaesthesia, no medicines — just instant, natural-looking results in 60–90 minutes.

How long does a hair patch last?

+
With proper care and regular maintenance, a high-quality hair patch can last 8 to 12 months. We provide complete guidance on cleaning, washing, and maintenance routines to maximize the lifespan of your hair system.

Will it look natural? Can anyone tell it's not my real hair?

+
Our hair patches and wigs are crafted from premium-quality human hair, meticulously matched to your natural hair colour, texture, and density. The result is virtually undetectable — even under bright lights or close inspection. We guarantee a seamless, natural look.

Can I shower, swim, and exercise with a hair patch?

+
Yes! Our hair systems are designed for active lifestyles. You can shower, swim, play sports, go to the gym, and even style your hair normally. The bonding is secure and water-resistant, so you can live your life freely without worrying about it coming loose.

What's the difference between hair bonding, hair fixing, and hair weaving?

+
Hair Bonding: Uses skin-friendly adhesives to bond the hair system. Most popular, natural-looking, and secure.

Hair Fixing: A broader term covering clip-on, tape, and adhesive methods for attaching hair patches and wigs.

Hair Weaving: Hair strands are woven or sewn onto a track system created from your existing hair. Best for those with some remaining hair who want volume.

How often do I need maintenance?

+
Regular maintenance is recommended every 3–5 weeks, depending on your lifestyle and the attachment method used. This includes scalp cleaning, re-bonding or re-taping, and professional styling. We offer affordable maintenance packages at our Ballygunge studio.

Is hair fixing better than hair transplant?

+
Hair fixing is non-surgical, painless, instant, and costs a fraction of hair transplant surgery. While transplants can cost ₹50,000–₹2,00,000 with unpredictable results and 6–12 month wait times, our hair fixing service gives you guaranteed, visible results same-day starting at just ₹5,999. No surgery, no scars, no regrets.

Who can benefit from hair fixing services?

+
Our hair fixing services are ideal for men dealing with: partial baldness, complete baldness, receding hairline, thinning hair, hair loss due to stress or genetics, and post-chemotherapy hair loss. If you're a man in Kolkata experiencing any form of hair loss, we have the right solution for you.
